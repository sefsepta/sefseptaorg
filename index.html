Welcome!

SEF SEPTA is the Special Education PTA for the communities of the Sewanhaka middle/high school district and the Elmont and Floral Park-Bellerose elementary school districts.

SEPTA meetings all take place on Wednesdays at 7:30 p.m. at Sewanhaka High School library. Here are the meeting dates for the 2018–2019 school year:

October 10, 2018
November 28, 2018
January 23, 2019
March 13, 2019
May 22, 2019
June 5, 2019
